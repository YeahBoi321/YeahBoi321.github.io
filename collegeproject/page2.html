Appalachian State University

Appalachian State is very large, 4-year, public university. It is a coed college that offers certificate, bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees. The graduation rate of App. State is 73%, it has a retention rate of 86%, and a student to faculty ratio of 16 to 1. 43.8% of students recieve financial aid with an average aid package of $9,611, with the University costing $10,525 after scholarships and grants.

Location

App. State is located in Boone, North Carolina. This is an Rural setting, and the campus is mainly residential. The housing at App. State costs an average of $9,681 every year. There are 5 housing options at App. State, and those include apartments for single students, coed housing, theme housing, coed housing, women's housing, and special housing for disabled students. There are 17,990 undergraduate students at App. State.

Mascot

Yosef, the Mascot

Yosef is the mascot of App. State. Yosef is mountian talk for "yourself", which you can hear if you say it the "right" way, "yos-e-f". Yosef was originally spelled with two f's, Yoseff, but it was changed to what we know today in January 1947. Yosef is a mascot beloved by most students at App. State,and originally, he wasn't even planned to become what he was today, which he wouldn't have been without the support of the students when he was first created.
